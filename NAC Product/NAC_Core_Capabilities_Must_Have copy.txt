Core Capability Must-Have List for NAC Solutions

1. Agentless device visibility and profiling
2. Support for wired NAC including 802.1X, MAB, and switch port control
3. Granular access control based on identity and posture
4. Integration with MDM platforms for compliance enforcement
5. Integration with EDR platforms for threat response
6. Posture assessment with automatic and manual remediation
7. API support for automation and external platform integration
8. Ability to consume third-party threat intelligence feeds
9. Local policy enforcement based on threat data
10. Scalable architecture with high availability support
11. Compliance reporting and audit capabilities
