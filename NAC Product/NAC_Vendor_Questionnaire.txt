Network Access Control (NAC) Vendor Questionnaire

Please provide detailed responses to the following questions to help us evaluate your NAC solution:

1. General Capabilities
   - Describe your NAC architecture (agent-based, agentless, hybrid).
   - What platforms and operating systems are supported?
   - How does your solution handle wired and wireless NAC?

2. Device Visibility and Profiling
   - What methods are used for device discovery and classification?
   - Can your solution identify IoT, OT, and medical devices?
   - How is device posture assessed?

3. Access Control and Policy Enforcement
   - Describe your support for 802.1X, MAC Authentication Bypass, and WebAuth.
   - How are policies defined and enforced?
   - Can your solution dynamically assign VLANs or ACLs?

4. Integration Capabilities
   - List supported MDM platforms and integration methods.
   - Describe integration with EDR, SIEM, SOAR, and threat intelligence platforms.
   - Can your solution consume third-party threat feeds and apply local policy?

5. Posture Assessment and Remediation
   - What posture agents are available (persistent, dissolvable, agentless)?
   - What posture conditions can be evaluated?
   - Describe remediation workflows (automatic/manual).

6. Behavioral Monitoring and Analytics
   - Does your solution support user behavior profiling?
   - Can it monitor transaction rates, data volumes, or application usage?
   - What external platforms are supported for analytics?

7. API and Automation
   - Describe available APIs (REST, GraphQL, Webhooks).
   - Can your solution integrate with transaction/data monitoring platforms via API?
   - Provide examples of automation workflows.

8. Threat Response
   - How does your solution respond to threat alerts from third-party platforms?
   - Can it quarantine or isolate endpoints based on threat intelligence?
   - Describe enforcement mechanisms (SGTs, dACLs, VLANs, scripts).

9. Scalability and Deployment
   - What is the recommended architecture for large environments?
   - How is high availability and redundancy handled?
   - Describe deployment complexity and onboarding process.

10. Compliance and Reporting
    - What compliance standards are supported (HIPAA, PCI-DSS, NIST)?
    - Describe reporting and audit capabilities.
    - Can users view and manage their compliance status?
